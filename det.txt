THEOREMX 2026: National Research Expo - Detailed Overview

What is THEOREMX 2026?
THEOREMX 2026 is a premier one-day National Research Expo organized by the Department of Data Science at Dr. M.G.R. Educational and Research Institute. Serving as a dynamic platform where "Research Meets Intelligence," this event is designed to bring together students, innovators, and researchers from across India to explore and showcase groundbreaking ideas in Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and emerging technologies.

Event Format and Venue
To ensure maximum accessibility, the expo will be hosted as a hybrid event, accommodating both online and offline participation. The physical event will take place on 24 June 2026 at the Dr. M.G.R. Educational and Research Institute campus located on Periyar E.V.R. High Road in Maduravoyal, Chennai.

Event Flow and Schedule
The day is structured to provide a comprehensive and engaging experience for all attendees:
• 09:00 – Registration & Welcome: Check-in, badges, and kit distribution for all participants.
• 10:00 – Opening Ceremony: Welcome address by the Dean and Department Head. Lighting of the lamp ceremony.
• 11:00 – Research Showcase Sessions Begin: Teams begin presenting their research papers and projects to the jury.
• 12:30 – AI Game Stalls Open: All interactive AI stalls open for all attendees to explore and participate.
• 01:30 – Lunch: Curated networking lunch with roundtable discussions and informal meetups.
• 02:30 – Final Presentations & Evaluation: Shortlisted teams present to the esteemed jury panel for final evaluation.
• 04:30 – Prize Distribution: Award ceremony recognizing top research, innovation, and AI game champions.
• 05:30 – Closing Ceremony: Closing remarks, group photos, and official wrap-up of THEOREMX 2026.

Who Can Participate?
The expo is open to:
• All UG and PG students from any college or university in India
• School students from Class X, XI, and XII
• Interdisciplinary teams are encouraged
Participants can compete individually or in teams of up to three members. To participate in the research showcase, teams must submit a 250–400 word abstract in IEEE format (including title, authors, objectives, methodology, and expected outcomes) before the deadline. Registration is completely FREE for everyone.
