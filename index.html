THEOREMX 2026 is open to all undergraduate and postgraduate students from any college or university across India. Interdisciplinary teams are encouraged.

Both individual and team participation is allowed. Teams can have up to 3 members. All members will receive individual certificates.

Abstracts should be 250–400 words in IEEE format. Download the official template from the Register section. Include title, authors, objectives, methodology, and expected outcomes.

Yes. Participation certificates are provided to all registered attendees. Merit certificates and awards are given to winners in all categories.

Registration details including fee (if applicable) will be announced closer to the event date. Participants from Dr. MGR ERI may be eligible for free registration.

An esteemed jury panel of faculty and industry experts will evaluate research quality, innovation, presentation clarity, and impact. The Audience Choice Award is voted by all attendees.

Bring your college ID, a printed or digital copy of your abstract, and your laptop if participating in tech stalls. Presenters should have their slides ready on a USB drive as backup.
